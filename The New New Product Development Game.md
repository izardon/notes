# new product development processes:

### 1. Built-in instability
高層主管給團隊一個具有挑戰性的目標或方向，讓團隊自己決定如何做，在尋找解答過程可能會有衝突，但也會激發更多創意。  
*Top management kicks off the development process by signaling a broad goal or a general strategic direction.*

### 2. Self-organizing project teams

A group possesses a self-organizing capability when it exhibits three conditions: autonomy, self-transcendence, and cross-fertilization. 

- Autonomy: 自治，高層提供環境與金錢給 project team 開發，不過度干涉
- Self-transcendence: 自我超越
- Cross-fertilization: 由具有不同職能專業，思維過程和行為模式的成員組成的項目團隊進行新產品開發

### 3. Overlapping development phases
自組織由不同職能專業的成員組成，通過重疊開發過程，成員們從"zero information"就開始一起工作、分享知識，可以從單獨的個體，更快的融合成一個整體

- 優點 (merits):
  - 硬優點是更快的速度與靈活性  
    *Greater speed and increased flexibility are the “hard” merits.*
  - 軟優點是人力資源管理有關的部分，增強了共同責任與合作，激發參與和投入，突出解決問題的重點，鼓勵主動，發展多樣技能，提高對市場的敏感性  
    *But the approach also has a set of “soft” merits relating to human resource management. The overlap approach enhances shared responsibility and cooperation, stimulates involvement and commitment, sharpens a problem-solving focus, encourages initiative taking, develops diversified skills, and heightens sensitivity toward market conditions.*
 

- 缺點 (demerits) :
  - 明顯的缺點是必須管理一個密集的過程。問題包括與整個項目團隊進行溝通，與供應商保持密切聯繫，準備一些應急計劃以及處理意外情況。 這種方法還會在小組中造成更多的緊張和衝突。  
    *The more obvious demerits result from having to manage an intensive process. Problems include communicating with the entire project team, maintaining close contact with suppliers, preparing several contingency plans, and handling surprises. This approach also creates more tension and conflict in the group.*

### 4. “Multilearning”

- Multilevel learning: 跨多個級別的學習(個人、團隊、公司)。
  - 在個人方面，組織應該保留一定比例的時間讓個人可以學習成長。
  - 在團隊層次，有時整個團隊需要集體抽離專案，看看其他團隊或公司以外的人怎麼做事情。
  - 在公司層面，如果公司定義清楚整個公司所要達成的目標或是能力，便可以做為團隊與個人的學習目標。 

- Multifunctional learning: 鼓勵在自己擅長以外的領域積累經驗，可以從團隊內擁有其他領域知識的成員上學習，藉此團隊成員可以從不同專業角度了解不同顧慮的面向，擁有更全面性的觀點，可以幫助自己做出更好的決策。  
  *Experts are encouraged to accumulate experience in areas other than their own.*

### 5. Subtle control (含蓄控制)
"自我控制"，"通過同伴壓力進行控制"，"通過愛進行控制"  
*The emphasis is on “self-control”, “control through peer pressure”, and “control by love”, which collectively we call “subtle control”.*

Subtle control is exercised in the new product development process in seven ways:

1. 選擇對的成員(能團隊合作、主動積極)，除掉不必要的成員。  
   *Selecting the right people for the project team while monitoring shifts in group dynamics and adding or dropping members when necessary.* 

2. 建立一個開放的共作環境。  
   *Creating an open work environment.*
    
3. 鼓勵工程師深入實地並聽取客戶的意見。  
   *Encouraging engineers to go out into the field and listen to what customers and dealers have to say.*

4. 建立基於團隊績效而非個人的考核與獎勵制度。  
   *Establishing an evaluation and reward system based on group performance.*

5. 管理開發步調，避免專案開始時轟轟烈烈，到後期卻無力了。  
   *Managing the differences in rhythm throughout the development process. As mentioned earlier, the rhythm is most vigorous in the early phases and tapers off toward the end.*

6. 容忍與預期犯錯。  
   *Tolerating and anticipating mistakes.*

7. 鼓勵供應廠商也採取自組織，在開發初期便邀請供應廠商參與，但避免告訴廠商該如何做，讓他們通過參與找出最佳解決方案。  
   Encouraging suppliers to become self-organizing. Involving them early during design is a step in the right direction. But the project team should refrain from telling suppliers what to do.
    
### 6. Organizational transfer of learning
成員將學習成果轉移給小組以外的其他人，意旨整個組織團隊與團隊之間的交流，可以透過將主要人員分配給後續項目。  
*The transfer took place through “osmosis”—by assigning key individuals to subsequent projects.*



---

- 自然，公司會嘗試將其成功經驗教訓制度化，但條件是需在穩定的外在環境下。
- 因此，有些公司會選擇忘記過去成功的經驗，並根據環境變化學習當下最好的方式。

# Some Limitations:
產品開發的整體方法無法在所有情況下適用，具有一些限制如下：  
*Some words of caution are in order. The holistic approach to product development may not work in all situations. It has some built-in limitations:*

1. 在整個開發過程中，所有成員都要付出巨大的努力。有時高峰時間為100小時，其實時間則為60小時。  
   *It requires extraordinary effort on the part of all project members throughout the span of the development process. Sometimes, team members record monthly overtime of 100 hours during the peak and 60 hours during the rest of the project.*

2. 不適用在需要革命性創新的突破項目，比如生物技術或化學等等。  
   *It may not apply to breakthrough projects that require a revolutionary innovation. This limitation may be particularly true in biotechnology or chemistry.*

3. 不適用於航空業這樣龐大的項目，因為龐大的項目規模限制了廣泛的面對面討論。  
   *It may not apply to mammoth projects like those in the aerospace business, where the sheer project scale limits extensive face-to-face discussions.*

4. 不適用於那些由發明家發明產品並提出了一套明確定義的規範以供以下人員遵循的天才組織。  
   *It may not apply to organizations where product development is masterminded by a genius who makes the invention and hands down a well-defined set of specifications for people below to follow.*

# Managerial Implications (管理意義)

為了實現速度和靈活性，公司必須對產品開發過程進行不同的管理。 應該考慮三種變化。  
*To achieve speed and flexibility, companies must manage the product development process differently. Three kinds of changes should be considered.*

#### 1.公司需要採用相對應的管理風格，新產品開發流程會是反覆 try and error 的方式，為了管理這樣的過程，公司必須保持高度適應性  
*First, companies need to adopt a management style that can promote the process. Executives must recognize at the outset that product development seldom proceeds in a linear and static manner. It involves an iterative and dynamic process of trial and error. To manage such a process, companies must maintain a highly adaptive style.*

- 設定具有挑戰性的目標，鼓勵 try and error。  
  Top management encourages trial and error by purposely keeping goals broad and by tolerating ambiguity. But at the same time, it sets challenging goals and creates tension within the group and within the organization.

- 在發展週期的各個重疊階段，都會發生品種被放大（分化）和減少（整合）的過程。 但是差異化往往會主導概念開發階段，而集成接管後續階段。  
  The process by which variety is amplified (differentiation) and reduced (integration) takes place throughout the overlapping phases of the development cycle. Differentiation, however, tends to dominate the concept development phase of the cycle, and integration begins to take over the subsequent phases.
    
- 操作決策是逐步的，但是重要的戰略決策會盡可能拖延，以便對市場反饋有更靈活的反映。  
  *Operational decisions are made incrementally, but important strategic decisions are delayed as much as possible in order to allow a more flexible response to last-minute feedback from the marketplace.*
    
#### 2.需要另一種學習方式  
*Second, a different kind of learning is required.*
- 傳統方法下，高品質的團隊進行新產品開發，成員都是由菁英組成，知識在聚焦在狹窄的區域(深度學習)，基於個人的累積。

- 新方法下，非專家負責產品開發，鼓勵他們在工作中獲得必要的知識與技能。專家無法在1％的時間內容忍錯誤，非專家們則願意挑戰現狀。 但是這樣做，他們必須從管理的各個領域，組織的不同級別，職能專業領域甚至組織邊界積累知識。 這種廣度的學習是共同分工有效發揮作用的必要條件。

#### 3.管理層應為新產品開發分配不同的任務。大多數公司主要將其視為未來收入來源的產生者。 但是在某些公司中，新產品開發也可以促進組織的變革  
*Third, management should assign a different mission to new product development. Most companies have treated it primarily as a generator of future revenue streams. But in some companies, new product development also acts as a catalyst to bring about change in the organization.*
